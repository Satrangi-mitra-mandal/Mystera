The Midnight Manor Incident
Victim: Victor Harrington, 58 · CEO, Harrington Capital
Victor Harrington was discovered dead in his private study at 3:14am on the morning of November 12th, 2026. The door was locked from the inside using a deadbolt. A glass of Scotch sat unfinished on his desk. The medical examiner's preliminary report suggests blunt force trauma, but traces of an unidentified compound were found in the bloodstream. Six guests were staying at Harrington Manor that weekend. By 3am, all claimed to be asleep.
