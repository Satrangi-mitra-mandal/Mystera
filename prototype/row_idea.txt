🕵️ Gamified Detective Cases Platform
1. Core Concept

Players become digital detectives who investigate cases through clues, evidence, and deduction.

Instead of just reading a story, they interact with evidence:

Police reports

Crime scene photos

Suspect interviews

Phone logs

Maps

Hidden clues

Goal: identify the culprit and explain the reasoning.

🧩 Gameplay Loop

1️⃣ Receive Case File

Background of the crime

Victim profile

Initial suspects

2️⃣ Explore Evidence

Documents

Photos

Forensic reports

Text messages

CCTV footage

3️⃣ Interrogate Suspects

Ask questions

Detect lies

Compare alibis

4️⃣ Connect Clues
Players create links like:

Knife → belongs to suspect A
Phone record → suspect B lied
Footprint → matches suspect C shoes

5️⃣ Submit Theory
Player answers:

Who did it?

Why?

How?

6️⃣ Score System
Points based on:

Correct culprit

Evidence used

Time taken

Logic accuracy
